Welcome!

I am an assistant professor at Université de Sherbrooke (Québec) Canada, doing research in robotics. My main interests are in human-robot interaction, software architectures for autonomous and distributed intelligent systems, and perception for collaborative robots at home and at work.

I am a member of IntRoLab at the Interdisciplinary Institute for Innovation (3IT).

You can learn about my ongoing research here, and feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
